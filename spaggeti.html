How to make Spaggeti

Spagetti

Spaggeti is a italian dish and my favorite food. It is not that hard at all. You need only a few things to get started.

Ingredients

  • Spaggeti Noodle
  • Red Sauce
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Put noodles in water for 30 mins and add a little salt
  3. Strain the noodles and add the red sauce, meat and cheese
