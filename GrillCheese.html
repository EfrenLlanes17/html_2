How to make a Grilled Cheese

Grilled Cheese

Grilled Cheese is a clasic and quick meal. You can be done with it in like 1 min. You need only a few things to get started.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter the Bread
  2. Put the Bread in the pan for 30 secs. Both sides(No cheese yet)
  3. Put the cheese in between the bread and press down on it.
  4. Leave it on the pan for 30 secs
