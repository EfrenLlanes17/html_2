How to make Lasagna

Lasagna

Lasagna is a italian dish that is kind of like spaggeti and pizz with meat. I have never cooked it but I am sure it is not that hard. You need only a few things to get started.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodle
  • Red Sauce
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees
  2. Make 4 Layers with each layer being like this: Noodle, Meat, Red Sauce, Cheese, Repeat
  3. Put in the oven for 2 hours
